Easygoing Manatee's Dive Site Cards

Every great snorkeler needs a deck of dive sites. These are Easygoing Manatee's top picks from around the world. Each card tells you everything you need to know before you splash in. 🌊

Caribbean

🐠 Blue Hole, Belize

Depth: 40 ft (snorkel) / 400 ft (dive)

Visibility: 100+ ft

Marine Life: Reef sharks, grouper, angelfish

Difficulty: Intermediate

Manatee Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Pacific

🐢 Hanauma Bay, Hawaii

Depth: 10–20 ft

Visibility: 60–80 ft

Marine Life: Sea turtles, parrotfish, eels

Difficulty: Beginner

Manatee Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Indian Ocean

🦈 Ningaloo Reef, Australia

Depth: 15–30 ft

Visibility: 80+ ft

Marine Life: Whale sharks, manta rays, coral

Difficulty: Beginner–Intermediate

Manatee Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Red Sea

🪸 Ras Mohammed, Egypt

Depth: 20–50 ft

Visibility: 100+ ft

Marine Life: Lionfish, barracuda, coral gardens

Difficulty: Intermediate

Manatee Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Florida

🐟 Crystal River, Florida

Depth: 5–15 ft

Visibility: 30–50 ft

Marine Life: Manatees (!!!), bass, catfish

Difficulty: Beginner

Manatee Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (personal fave 🐾)

Southeast Asia

🦑 Tubbataha Reef, Philippines

Depth: 20–60 ft

Visibility: 100+ ft

Marine Life: Hammerhead sharks, turtles, rays

Difficulty: Advanced

Manatee Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
